MCQ GOD Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

MCQ GOD is a phone-first study app for turning class materials and exam questions into answer cards with explanations and proof.

Information You Choose To Add

  • Files you select, such as PDFs, notes, slides, transcripts, images, screenshots, and old exams.
  • Photos you take inside the app, such as question pages or class material.
  • Optional AI API keys that you paste into the app for prototype mode.

How The App Uses This Information

  • To read your selected study materials and extract questions.
  • To generate answer cards, explanations, and source references.
  • To show file-reading and answer progress on your device.
  • To save local app settings, such as your selected AI mode and pasted API key.

AI Processing

In prototype mode, MCQ GOD may send selected text, images, or study questions to the AI provider you configure in the app, such as Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or another provider. This is used only to read material, answer questions, and explain results. Do not add information that you do not want processed by the selected AI provider.

Storage

The current Android prototype is local-first. It does not require a developer-run account or cloud workspace. Files, generated cards, and pasted API keys are stored in the app/browser storage on your device unless you delete them or clear the app data.

Sharing

MCQ GOD does not sell personal information. When you use AI features, selected study content may be transmitted to the AI provider you chose so that the provider can return the requested answer, summary, or explanation.

Permissions

  • Camera access is used for scanning question pages and study material.
  • File picker access is used only for files you select.
  • Network access is used for optional AI provider requests and normal app functionality.

Deletion

You can delete locally stored study data and settings by using the app's clear/reset options where available, by clearing the app's storage in Android settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children

MCQ GOD is intended for high school, college, and adult learners. It is not directed to children under 13.
